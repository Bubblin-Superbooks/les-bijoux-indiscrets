Chapter XXXIII.

Sixteenth Trial Of The Ring, Or The petits-maitres.

Twice a week the favorite kept a drawing room. The preceding evening she named the women whom she would willingly see, and the Sultan gave the list of the men. The company always came richly dress'd.
